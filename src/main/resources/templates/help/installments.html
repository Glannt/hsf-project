Installment Payment Guide

This page explains how our installment payments work. Choose one of the available plans and pay over time.

How it works
  1. Select an installment plan during checkout. The monthly amount depends on the plan duration and interest rate.
  2. Each month, pay the amount due before the due date shown in your schedule.
  3. You can review all active plans in your account page and track paid or upcoming installments.
  4. Late payments may incur penalties and restrict new plans.
